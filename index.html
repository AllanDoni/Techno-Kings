    • About Us

    TECHNO KINGS Symposium holds both technical and non-technical events. We are glad to invite students from all departments in the domain of engineering, Especially the students from Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Mechanical Engineering (MECH) and Information Technology (IT). The students from other departments are also most welcome to participate in the Symposium.
    The events are aspired to bring out the innovative and creative potential of the young budding engineers. Also, the students can able to uncover their unique talents in the non-technical field too. The main objective of the symposium is to encourage and expose their knowledge in technical level and also to exhibit their talents in non-technical arena too.
